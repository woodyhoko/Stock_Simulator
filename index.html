Agent-Based Computational Finance

100 AI traders.
Price emerges from chaos.

Two competing species of neural-network traders buy and sell a single asset over 300 rounds. The price isn't imposed — it's endogenous, formed by aggregate order pressure. Profit means survival; bankruptcy means replacement by mutated offspring. Real market phenomena emerge from the bottom up.

By Ho Ko — CSIE, National Central University

four brains
A heterogeneous trading ecology.
[ MLP ]

Deep learning

An online-trained perceptron over price returns and moving-average ratios; weights updated by last-action PnL.

[ SVM ]

Support vector machine

RBF-kernel classifier splitting buy from sell on momentum / RSI / mean-reversion features.

[ HOPFIELD ]

Associative memory

Stores historical price patterns as attractors; converges to the nearest to predict the next move.

[ NEAT ]

Evolving topology

Evolves both weights and network structure — growing complexity only when the market warrants it.
