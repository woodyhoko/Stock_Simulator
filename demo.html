Stock Simulator · 100 Evolving AI Traders

100 AI agents trade two products, A and B, in a market whose prices emerge from their own buy/sell pressure. Each agent's strategy is a small genome (momentum vs. mean-reversion tilt per product, plus risk). Every trading season the weakest half is culled and the survivors breed mutated offspring — a genetic algorithm, so only good strategies persist. Watch the evolution curve climb as the gene cloud converges.
